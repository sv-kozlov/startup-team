# Resource and Budget Control

## Purpose

Track resource and budget constraints that affect delivery commitments.

## Use When

- Budget, resource, capacity, vendor, or cost constraints affect plan.
- Scope changes need delivery cost or resource impact.
- Leadership needs budget or staffing visibility.

## Inputs

- Budget baseline, staffing plan, vendor commitments, estimates, actuals, scope changes.

## Workflow

1. State baseline and current variance.
2. Identify resource or budget driver.
3. Link impact to scope, date, quality, or risk.
4. Identify owner and decision needed.
5. Escalate options with consequences.

## Outputs

- Resource and budget control note.
- Variance summary.
- Decision options.

## Boundaries

- Does not invent cost, availability, or estimates.
- Does not own HR, compensation, procurement policy, or financial accounting.
- Does not decide product priority without Product Manager.
