# Dependency Management

## Purpose

Identify, track, and coordinate dependencies that affect delivery.

## Use When

- Work depends on another team, vendor, environment, decision, or artifact.
- Dependency status threatens schedule, quality, or scope.
- Teams need a shared dependency map.

## Inputs

- Workstreams, milestones, owners, artifacts, external teams, constraints, dates.

## Workflow

1. List dependencies and classify by owner and type.
2. Identify required artifact, decision, or action.
3. Record due date, risk, current status, and escalation path.
4. Review dependency impact on plan.
5. Hand off content decisions to owner roles.

## Outputs

- Dependency map.
- Dependency register.
- Escalation list.

## Boundaries

- Does not own the content of product, requirements, architecture, QA, or DevOps dependencies.
- Does not invent dates or commitments without owner confirmation.
