# Stakeholder Communications

## Purpose

Plan and execute project communications that reduce delivery risk and support decisions.

## Use When

- Stakeholder groups need different update formats or cadences.
- Communication gaps create risk or duplicated work.
- An escalation or decision request must be framed.

## Inputs

- Stakeholders, goals, risks, decisions, information needs, cadence, channels.

## Workflow

1. Identify contributors, decision makers, beneficiaries, and impacted groups.
2. Define what each group needs to know and decide.
3. Set cadence, channel, owner, and artifact.
4. Prepare clear status, risk, and decision messages.
5. Record feedback and resulting actions.

## Outputs

- Stakeholder communication plan.
- Escalation message.
- Decision communication note.

## Boundaries

- Does not own product positioning, marketing campaign, sales communication, or support scripts.
- Does not replace Business Analyst stakeholder analysis for business process ownership.
