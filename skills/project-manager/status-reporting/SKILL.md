# Status Reporting

## Purpose

Report delivery status clearly enough for decisions, risk management, and stakeholder trust.

## Use When

- Leadership or stakeholders need project status.
- A steering committee or status meeting needs material.
- Delivery health must be summarized by scope, schedule, budget, risk, and decisions.

## Inputs

- Plan, actual progress, milestones, risks, issues, dependencies, budget, scope changes.

## Workflow

1. State current status against baseline.
2. Show changes since last update.
3. Highlight risks, issues, dependencies, and decisions needed.
4. Name owners and dates for next actions.
5. Keep product and technical content owned by the relevant role.

## Outputs

- Status report.
- Executive summary.
- Decision request.
- Escalation note.

## Boundaries

- Does not obscure bad news or owner gaps.
- Does not make product or technical decisions through status language.
