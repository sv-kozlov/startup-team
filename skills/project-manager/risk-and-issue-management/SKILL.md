# Risk and Issue Management

## Purpose

Make delivery risks and issues visible, owned, mitigated, and escalated before they damage project outcomes.

## Use When

- Risks, issues, blockers, or escalations need structure.
- A project needs a risk register or mitigation plan.
- Leadership needs options and impact.

## Inputs

- Project objective, constraints, dependencies, current blockers, probability, impact, owners.

## Workflow

1. Separate risk from issue.
2. State impact on date, budget, scope, quality, or stakeholder commitment.
3. Assign owner, mitigation, contingency, and due date.
4. Define escalation trigger and decision needed.
5. Review regularly and close only with evidence.

## Outputs

- Risk register.
- Issue log.
- Mitigation and escalation plan.

## Boundaries

- Does not solve technical, product, QA, or operational content as owner.
- Does not hide uncertainty or owner gaps.
