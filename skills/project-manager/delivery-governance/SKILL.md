# Delivery Governance

## Purpose

Define how delivery work is controlled, reviewed, escalated, and kept transparent.

## Use When

- A project needs governance model or steering rhythm.
- Decision rights, escalation path, or reporting cadence are unclear.
- Multiple teams or vendors need delivery control.

## Inputs

- Stakeholders, workstreams, decision owners, risks, cadence, reporting needs.

## Workflow

1. Identify governance layers and decision owners.
2. Define meeting cadence, artifacts, status inputs, and escalation triggers.
3. Map decisions, risks, and dependencies to forums.
4. Record roles and expectations.
5. Keep content ownership with Product, Analysis, Engineering, QA, and DevOps roles.

## Outputs

- Governance model.
- Decision and escalation map.
- Meeting and reporting cadence.

## Boundaries

- Does not own product, requirements, technical, QA, or operational decisions.
- Does not create bureaucracy without decision value.
