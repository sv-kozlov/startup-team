# Release and Implementation Coordination

## Purpose

Coordinate delivery-side release, implementation, rollout, and transition activities across teams.

## Use When

- Release or go-live needs coordination across Product, QA, DevOps, Support, and business.
- Implementation has dependencies, approvals, training, vendor work, or transition tasks.
- Cutover or rollback risk needs project-side control.

## Inputs

- Release scope, readiness inputs, teams, environments, support model, dates, risks, approvals.

## Workflow

1. Confirm scope and release objective with Product Manager and Product Owner.
2. Map readiness inputs from QA, DevOps, Support, business, and vendors.
3. Define milestones, owners, go/no-go criteria, and rollback/escalation path.
4. Track blockers and decisions.
5. Record post-release actions and transition ownership.

## Outputs

- Release coordination plan.
- Implementation checklist.
- Go/no-go decision brief.
- Transition handoff.

## Boundaries

- Does not own deployment execution, QA sign-off, support operations, or product launch decision.
- Does not rewrite product scope or acceptance criteria.
